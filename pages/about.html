About us

Teaching babies to love
the water since 2015

Blue Fin Swim School started with one pool, one instructor, and one mission: help every child in Durban learn to be safe and confident in the water.

Our story

From a backyard pool to a school families trust

What started as weekend classes in a backyard pool in Berea grew into Durban's most-loved infant swim school. Founder Sara Molefe began teaching after seeing a near-drowning incident at a family gathering — and decided every child in her community should know how to be safe around water.

Today Blue Fin runs structured classes six days a week, with two certified instructors, a maximum class size of four children, and a curriculum built around age-specific developmental milestones.

Safety

Our safety standards

Every class is designed with child safety as the primary concern.

Medically cleared instructors
All instructors are First Aid certified and hold up-to-date CPR training. Recertified annually.
4:1 maximum ratio
Never more than 4 children per instructor. A hard cap enforced by the booking system.
Consent & medical forms
Every child requires a signed consent form before their first class. Medical notes shared with instructors before each session.
